About Us

Harvestify

At Harvestify, we empower farmers and plant enthusiasts with AI-driven insights to support smarter agricultural decisions. Our platform allows users to upload images of their plants, and in return, it accurately predicts the plant's growth stage using advanced machine learning techniques. Based on the detected stage, Harvestify also provides personalized fertilizer recommendations to ensure optimal growth and yield. We're committed to making modern farming more efficient, sustainable, and accessible through the power of technology.
